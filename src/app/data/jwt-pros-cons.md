question: What are the pros and cons of JWT?
tags: [security, jwt]
difficulty: medium
keywords: [JWT, Pros, Cons, Stateless, Authentication, Authorization, Token]
---
JWTs are a stateless way to handle authentication.
They reduce the need for server-side session storage.
JWTs are easy to use across multiple services.
A major downside is that they cannot be easily revoked.
Storing sensitive data inside JWTs is unsafe.
Token expiration must be handled carefully.
