Agile development is an iterative approach to building software.
It focuses on delivering small, usable increments frequently.
Requirements and solutions evolve through collaboration.
Agile values working software over detailed documentation.
It emphasizes responding to change rather than following a fixed plan.
Common agile frameworks include Scrum and Kanban.
